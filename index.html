History team

On January 18, 2016, the Danish team takes a new tag called Astralis. Initially, the name belonged to a Finnish organization, but due to the efforts of the manager, the players got a resounding name. The organization was originally owned by the former Danish SoloMid / Questionmark team. However, the rights were later bought by the parent company Astralis Group.


Team Questionmark has launched a new lineup for Astralis. The players of the team completely moved under the new banners. That composition was little different from the current one, however, few people knew that karrigan was the founder of Astralis. Players such as Xyp9x, dupreeh, dev1ce and cajunb joined him in the new team. And so begins the story of the Astralis team, which raised the whole modern Counter-Strike.
